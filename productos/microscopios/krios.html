The 300 kV Thermo Scientiﬁc Krios 5 Cryo-Transmission Electron Microscope (Cryo-TEM) has been designed for high-performance single particle analysis (SPA), cellular cryo-electron tomography (cryo-ET) and microcrystal electron diﬀraction (MicroED).
The Krios 5 Cryo-TEM is capable of quickly producing high-quality structures thanks to a unique focus on high throughput with high data ﬁdelity, increased automation of routine alignments, improved optical precision, and AI-enabled software, which make it a highly productive and “ready-to-use” system for all expertise levels across multiple scientiﬁc ﬁelds.
Experience up to 25% faster throughput relative to previous Krios Cryo-TEM generations with high data fidelity for single particle analysis.
Extended aberration-free image shift (AFIS) accelerates data collection, while AI-powered Thermo Scientific Smart EPU Software plugins automate routine alignments.
Thermo Scientific Tomography 5 Software provides high image quality via automatic 8K Electron Event Representation (EER) decoding and on-the-fly motion correction for crisp tomograms, unlocking the full potential of the Thermo Scientific Falcon 4i Direct Electron Detector beyond the Nyquist limit.
Easily transfer Autogrid cassettes between Autoloaders contamination-free with the Thermo Scientific Vacuum Capsule.
Designed for sustainability, the Krios 5 Cryo-TEM features energy-saving Eco modes and environmental footprint traceability with a My Green Lab ACT Ecolabel.
